# Growth Framework

My approach is built around four principles: objective, alignment, optimisation, and data.

📈
Clear objectives drive every campaign

I start with a clear business objective, whether that is leads, conversions, or revenue, so every campaign stays tied to business impact.

🎯
Audience, message, and channel must align

Performance improves when the offer, creative, and platform match the audience. Strong alignment is what turns activity into results.

🧪
Test, learn, and optimise continuously

I test early, review performance closely, and improve the parts that move results, from targeting and creative to landing pages.

📊
Data drives every decision

I use data to spot what is helping or hurting performance, then make practical decisions based on what the numbers are saying.
